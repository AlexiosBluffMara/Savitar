# Research Notes — Illinois Grants and Small Business Programs

**Last updated:** 2026-04-07  
**Source:** Web research, official government sites, Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce

---

## Illinois State-Level Grants

### Neighborhood Opportunity Fund (City of Chicago)
- **Amount:** Up to $250K (small projects), $250K–$2.5M (large projects)
- **Eligibility:** Businesses on Chicago's West, South, Southwest commercial corridors
- **Restrictions:** Small projects must be on qualifying corridors; large projects have no regional restrictions
- **Deadline:** Rolling
- **URL:** chicago.gov (Neighborhood Opportunity Fund)

### SBIR/STTR Phase I Matching Grant (State of Illinois)
- **Amount:** Up to $50,000 per project (state match)
- **Eligibility:** Illinois-based federal SBIR/STTR Phase I recipients
- **Purpose:** Increase capital for small businesses doing cutting-edge research
- **Deadline:** Rolling (tied to federal SBIR/STTR awards)

### Illinois Small Business Emergency Loan Fund
- **Amount:** Up to $50,000
- **Terms:** 5-year repayment, 6-month deferred payment
- **Eligibility:** Illinois small businesses facing financial hardship
- **Scale:** Over $40M allocated to 1,200+ businesses

### Advantage Illinois Program
- Sub-programs: loan participation, capital access, collateral support
- Designed for growth and expansion businesses

### Back to Business (B2B) Grant Program
- **Total distributed:** $250M+ to 6,500+ businesses
- **Focus:** COVID recovery, business stabilization, staff retention
- **Note:** May be winding down, check current status

### Federal Grant Match Program (DCEO)
- **Amount:** $15 million total allocation
- **Eligibility:** Illinois-based businesses pursuing competitive federal grants
- **Administered by:** Illinois Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity

## Chicago-Specific Programs

### We Rise Together
- **Amount:** Up to $12,500
- **Partners:** We Rise Together, Allies for Community Business, Small Business Advisory Council
- **Purpose:** Operating grants for businesses expanding into storefronts

### Small Business Improvement Fund (SBIF)
- Reimbursement grants for permanent building improvements
- City-wide eligibility

### Community Development Grants (CDG)
- Small, Medium, and Large tiers
- Support business and neighborhood vitality

### Small Business Storefront Activation Program
- Grants for community and economic development organizations

### Diversity Credit Program
- Increases use of minority and women-owned businesses on private sector contracts

### Energy Efficiency Trust Fund Grant
- Support energy efficiency projects in environmental justice areas
- Residential electric customer focus

## National Minority Business Programs

### MBDA (Minority Business Development Agency)
- FY23 stats: $1.5B capital facilitated, $3.8B contracts, 19,000+ jobs
- National network of Business Centers and Specialty Centers
- Technical assistance for MBEs (Minority Business Enterprises)

### NMSDC Business Consortium Fund
- Supports certified minority-owned businesses
- Requires MBE certification through NMSDC

### PRIME (Program for Investment in Micro-Entrepreneurs)
- Grants with priority for women-owned and socially/economically disadvantaged
- Multiple grant categories

### CDFIs (Community Development Financial Institutions)
- Grant and loan programs for underrepresented communities
- Fund minority entrepreneurs and mission-driven organizations

### Comcast RISE
- $10,000 grants for women- or minority-owned businesses
- Includes: technical assistance, marketing support, free internet, coaching
- Quarterly application cycles

### Amber Grant for Women (WomensNet)
- $10,000 monthly to 3 recipients (judged)
- Monthly winners eligible for $25,000 year-end bonus
- Total potential: $35,000

## Illinois Community College Board (ICCB)

### Active Grant Programs
1. **Advancing Developmental Education Reform** — up to $50K/institution, faculty capacity building
2. **ESL Services Expansion** — for Title II programs serving asylees, refugees, migrants
3. **Adult Education and Literacy PD Network** — $360K total for professional development
4. **FY26 Renewal programs** — ongoing cycle

### University of Illinois System
- **Illinois Innovation Network (IIN)** — AI workforce development funding
- **NASA Space Grant** — AI workforce focus in aerospace
- **UIUC OVCRI** — Data and AI workforce development grants (limited submission)
