## Illinois State University (Normal, IL)

### Center for Civic Engagement (CCE)
- **Collaboratory** — ISU's official database of community partner activities. Savitar could help faculty and staff report civic engagement activities, search for existing partnerships, and coordinate across departments.
- **Community Consulting Board** — advisors from local organizations who guide campus-community partnerships. Savitar as a shared tool between campus and partner organizations.
- **Community as Co-Educator** — community org staff expand their skills while educating students. A shared agent that both groups can query is a natural extension.
- **Grants for Civic Engagement** — CCE offers funding for civic engagement initiatives. A Savitar-assisted grant-writing workflow is directly applicable.
- **Contact:** Center for Civic Engagement, (309) 438-1100, ceceserve@IllinoisState.edu

### Partnership Angles for Duo Submission
1. **Student interns** build and test memory packs for ISU-specific knowledge
2. **Faculty research** on AI-assisted civic engagement and community knowledge systems
3. **Capstone projects** in CS or IT that extend Savitar transports or web UI
4. **CCE reporting** — Savitar helps track and report civic engagement activities to Collaboratory
5. **Cross-campus collaboration** — departments share subject-area memory packs through a common Savitar instance

### ISU Computer Science / IT Programs
- School of Information Technology (IT) — applied focus, good fit for capstone integration
- Department of Computer Science — research focus, potential for academic evaluation
- ISU has a strong community-engagement identity under its Civic Engagement classification

## National Minority Business Programs

### Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA)
- Federal agency promoting growth of Minority Business Enterprises (MBEs)
- FY23: facilitated $1.5B in capital, $3.8B in contracts, 19,000+ jobs
- MBDA Business Centers provide technical assistance
- Savitar memory pack: MBDA program details, application processes, success metrics

### National Minority Supplier Development Council (NMSDC)
- Business Consortium Fund supports certified minority-owned businesses
- Certification pathway for MBE status
- Savitar use case: guide businesses through NMSDC certification

### Program for Investment in Micro-Entrepreneurs (PRIME)
- Grants to small businesses with priority for women-owned and socially/economically disadvantaged
- Savitar use case: eligibility assessment and application guidance

### Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs)
- Grants and programs for underrepresented communities
- Fund minority entrepreneurs and mission-driven organizations
- Savitar use case: CDFI directory and program matching

### Comcast RISE
- $10,000 grants for women- or minority-owned businesses
- Includes technical assistance, marketing support, free internet, coaching
- Savitar use case: application guidance and deadline tracking

## Apple Education as a Deployment Accelerator

Apple Education pricing is available year-round to:
- K-12 schools and school districts
- Community colleges (all 48 ICCB institutions)
- Universities (ISU, UIUC, etc.)
- Faculty and staff (personal purchases)
- Students with .edu verification

**Mac Mini M4 education price: $499** (vs $599 retail) — or $41.58/month for 12 months.

This means:
- An ISU department can deploy for under $500
- A community college can fund it from a single ICCB grant ($50K covers 100 Mac Minis)
- A high school CS class can justify it as lab equipment
- The seasonal Back to School promotion adds free peripherals

Apple Education isn't just a discount — it's an existing procurement channel that every school's IT department already knows how to use. Savitar doesn't need to create a new purchasing process; it rides an existing one.

## Google Ecosystem as the Mobile Layer

The Apple-side story (Mac Mini + iMessage) is complemented by the Google-side story:

- **Pixel 9 Pro Fold** — mobile operator dashboard, 8" folding screen for presentations
- **Google Fi** — $20–$65/mo connectivity, international coverage for WhatsApp, Fi number as WhatsApp Business endpoint
- **Tensor G4 + LiteRT** — future path for on-device Gemma 4 E2B inference
- **Google login** — web UI authentication uses Google OAuth
- **Gemini Advanced** — ships free with Pixel 9 Pro Fold for 1 year, complementary to Savitar's Gemma 4 stack

This dual-ecosystem coverage (Apple infrastructure + Google mobile) is uncommon in hackathon entries and demonstrates that Savitar is platform-agnostic by design.
